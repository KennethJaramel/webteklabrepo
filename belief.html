Religion is a cultural system of behaviours and practices, world views, sacred texts, holy places, ethics, and societal organisation that relate humanity.

It is also a particular system of faith and worship.

It is also a set of beliefs concerning the cause, nature, and purpose of the universe, especially, when considered as the creation of a superhuman agency or agencies, usually involving devotional and ritual observances, and often containing a moral code governing the doncut of human affairs.

According to some estimates, there are roughly 4,200 religions in the world.

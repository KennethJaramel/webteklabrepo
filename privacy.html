For all human beings we have rights to have a privacy between our friends and family. And we have all the rights to have a protection of the law if we are in bad situation.

According to Article 17:

1. No one have the right to do bad interference between his/her privacy, home, family or correspondence, nor to the unlawful attacks on his/her honour and reputation.

2. No one should interfere the privacy of each family.

3. No one have rights to go to one house if there is no permission of the home owner.

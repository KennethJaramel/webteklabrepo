Human rights is an equity for all human beings. A right to protect the dignity of each person. It is also the rights that people all have whether we have different religion, gender, origin, province, country, color, status, region or language. And a right that is beleived to belong to every person.

Human rights are rights that are provided for people. Commonly understood as being those rights which are inherent to the human being. It is a right that is moral and legal entitlement to the welfare and benefit of the general populace. These are set of laws that will protect each person from unequal treatment and discrimination.

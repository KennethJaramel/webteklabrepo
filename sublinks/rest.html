It is the right for every living person to be able to have rest and holidays as much as anybody is able to.

This is because it is, first of all, only normal for anyone to rest when they are tired. If they are not able to, it would rob the people of their right which could lead to them being unable to perform their duties and in the worse case, might make them grow weaker or catch a sickness.

However, this could also lead people to abuse this right as well. Too much rest would make the person grow truant or lax and also be unable to perform their duties in society. Therefore, like with any other right, it would be best if it is done in moderation.

This right is something that is understood without it having be explicitly stated anywhere. It is already a fact that governments around the world put up laws that allow every worker or laborer to be able to rest or get a holiday.
